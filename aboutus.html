Meet The Founders:
Hi! We are both students at the University of California, Riverside. We started this organization as a club that raises awareness around women’s health and education, as we feel it is a topic that is not properly explained in high schools. We hope to be a resource and a form of guidance and support for high schools around California to amplify their sex ed curriculum and make it more inclusive and topical in today’s generation.
